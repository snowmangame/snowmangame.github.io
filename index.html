Hangman

Hangman is a word guessing game where one player thinks of a word, or a phrase. The other will try and guess the word by suggesting letters within a certain number of guesses. The word to guess is usually represented by a row of dashes, which represents the letters of a word. In this edition, the same rules apply. Except that instead of a guy getting neck-roped, You have to not let the snowman (called Snowie) melt. If you do, you get coal for Christmas. Here is the link for the game: Snowman!

Also, there will be a home button at the top-left if you want to go back here.
